Arroz con Leche

Arroz con leche is a creamy, comforting rice pudding made by simmering rice with milk, sugar, and cinnamon

Ingredients

  • Arroz Con Leche Ingredients
    • White rice Whole milk
    • Water
    • Cinnamon stick (canela)
    • Sugar
    • Vanilla extract
    • Sweetened condensed milk

Directions

  1. Rinse the rice until the water runs mostly clear
  2. In a pot, cook the rice with water and a cinnamon stick until the rice is tender and water is mostly absorbed
  3. Add whole milk (and condensed milk if using) and stir gently
  4. Simmer on low heat, stirring often, until creamy and thick
  5. Add sugar, vanilla, and a small pinch of salt
  6. Cook a few more minutes until fully smooth and comforting
  7. Remove the cinnamon stick and serve warm or chilled
